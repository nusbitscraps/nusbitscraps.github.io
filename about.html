About Bitscraps

Bits to Brilliance

🦖Welcome to Bitscraps🦖 - your gateway to the fascinating world of repair and sustainability! We are a dynamic interest group open to all faculties and majors, dedicated to empowering students with the essential skills needed for repair and make a positive impact on the environment.

At Bitscraps, we believe in the power of hands-on learning and real life applications of engineering. Our weekly lab sessions are designed to teach you vital technical skills such as soldering🧑‍🔬👩‍🔬, debugging🖥💻, and circuit analysis⚡️🔌, all while fostering a collaborative and stress-free environment. As a member, you will have the opportunity to put your newfound knowledge to the test by fixing broken electronics yourself!

Whether you're a seasoned electronics enthusiast or a complete beginner regardless of your background: engineering, business or even a literature major, Bitscraps welcomes you with open arms!🫲😊🫱

Join us in our mission to spread the ‘repair before replace’ attitude. Together, we can make a real difference, one circuit at a time. Sign ups will open again at the start of next semester!🦖🦖🦖
